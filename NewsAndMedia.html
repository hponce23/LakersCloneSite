Kobe’s legacy immortalized: Kobe Statue Unveiling 2.8.24

On 2.8.24, fans can forever pay tribute to beloved Lakers Legend and five-time champion Kobe Bryant. The Los Angeles Lakers and Vanessa Bryant announced today that they will unveil Kobe Bryant’s bronze statue at Star Plaza outside of Crypto.com Arena on Feb. 8, 2024, prior to the Lakers’ home game that evening.

Lakers Announce 2023-24 Season Schedule

The Los Angeles Lakers announced their 2023-24 regular season schedule, presented by Delta Air Lines. The Lakers will tip off the new season on the road against Denver on Tuesday, Oct. 24, marking a rematch of the 2023 Western Conference Finals. Los Angeles will open its home slate on Thursday, Oct. 26, versus Phoenix at Crypto.com Arena.

Max Christie Named to NBA 2K24 All-Summer League Second Team

Christie appeared in three games for the Lakers at NBA 2K24 Summer League in Las Vegas, averaging 19.0 points (45.7 FG%, 50.0 3P%, 100.0 FT%), 6.3 rebounds, 3.7 assists and 2.3 blocks in 32.2 minutes per game. Against Golden State on July 7, the 20-year-old finished with 22 points, seven rebounds, two assists and two blocks in 31 minutes. Christie followed up that performance with 11 points, four rebounds, six assists and four blocks in 31 minutes against Charlotte on July 9. In his final game of the summer against Boston on July 12, Christie posted 24 points, eight rebounds, three assists, two steals and one block in 35 minutes.
